My Hobbies:

  • Reading
  • Playing viola
  • Dance

Reading is enjoyable because I am able to "travel" to new worlds and meet new characters. Moreover, reading is a great way to relax because you are able to carry a book anywhere. Reading is also proven to improve vocabulary and reduce stress.

I enjoy playing the viola because I am able to play my own music. Additionally, playing music is something you can do with anyone because there is no language barrier. Furthermore, I enjoy playing the viola because you never stop learning how to play because there is always something new to discover.

Dancing is fun because you can make new friends, learn new skills, and exercise your body. Personally, dance is fun for me because I get to see my friends and dance with them at competitions and recitals.
